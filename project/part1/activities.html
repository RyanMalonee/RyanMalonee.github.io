Attractions

Fun Land

A great amusement park to enjoy rides, play games, and have a good time!

Go Ape!

A fun zip-lining course for you and your family to enjoy.

Shell We Golf

A cute mini-golfing location right near Rehoboth beach.

Winterthur Museum & Gardens

A beautiful museum and garden on Henry Francis du Pont's property.

Jungle Jim's

A large waterpark located in Rehoboth, DE. With so much to do, the family is sure to have fun!
