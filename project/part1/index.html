Navigating the State

Delaware is a very small state (only about 2.5 hours from the northern-most to southern-most points. Comprised of three different counties you can easily get anywhere you want to via car. Depending on what you're looking for, one might want to spend their time in one county over the other. New Castle County is home to the Christiana Mall and the City of Wilminton, both great places to shop with Delaware's tax free shopping. Kent County is the home of Dover, the state's capital filled with exciting history, and Sussex Country is the home of the beaches, with great views and a relaxing vibe you are destined to have fun.
