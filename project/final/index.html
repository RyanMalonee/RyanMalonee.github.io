Delaware

As the first state of the United States of America, Delaware offers visitors a unique historical experiences filled with fun activities and endless attractions. There is so much to do there that it'll be hard to get bored. With three counties, and many different attractions, activities, and historical locations sprinkled throughout, you'll fall in love with the state, just like I did.

As we explore the state, you'll be able to see some of the most popular attractions and activities that Delaware has to offer. You'll also be able to see some of the most beautiful pictures of the state, and learn a little bit about me and my experience in the state. I've lived in Delaware for over 16 years and I'm excited to share the state with you.

Navigating the State

Delaware is a very small state (only about 2.5 hours from the northern-most to southern-most points). Comprised of three different counties you can easily get anywhere you want to via car. Depending on what you're looking for, one might want to spend their time in one county over the other. New Castle County is home to the Christiana Mall and the City of Wilmington, both great places to shop with Delaware's tax free shopping. Kent County is the home of Dover, the state's capital filled with exciting history, and Sussex Country is the home of the beaches, with great views and a relaxing vibe you are destined to have fun.
