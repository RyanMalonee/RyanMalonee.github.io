Attractions

<

Fun Land

A great amusement park to enjoy rides, play games, and have a good time!

Go Ape!

A fun zip-lining course for you and your family to enjoy.

Shell We Golf

A cute mini-golfing location right near Rehoboth beach.

Winterthur Museum & Gardens

A beautiful museum and garden on Henry Francis du Pont's property.

Jungle Jim's

A large waterpark located in Rehoboth, DE. With so much to do, the family is sure to have fun!

Activities

<

Beach Day

Delaware has many beautiful beaches to go to. No matter which one you end up at it will be a good time.

Explore the Parks

In addition to beaches, Delaware has 18 state parks ready to be explored!

Shopping Trip

Delaware is one of the few states that offer no sales tax. So explore the outlets or the malls to shop!

Visit Historic New Castle

New Castle is a historic town with an old vibe. It offers many museums to check out.

Explore Fort Delaware

Fort Delaware is located on Pea Patch Island and is a Civil-War era fortress with an interesting history.
